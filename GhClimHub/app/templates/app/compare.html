Brief Overview of Indices

NDVI anomaly is the difference between the average NDVI for a particular month of a given year and the average NDVI for the same month over a specified number of years. This approach can be used to characterize the health of vegetation for a particular month and year relative to what is considered normal, which is a good indicator of drought or declining vegetation health
